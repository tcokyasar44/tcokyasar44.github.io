Research Interests

    Designing and applying mathematical and computational models to optimize location, routing, scheduling, and resource allocation in transportation networks. This includes innovative approaches for vehicle routing problems, electric vehicle charging, and last-mile delivery logistics.

    Developing data-driven models to optimize freight movement and public transit operations, improving service reliability, cost-effectiveness, and overall system performance.

  • Data Analytics & Simulation for Transportation
    Leveraging large-scale datasets and simulation techniques to evaluate transportation policies, infrastructure investments, and emerging mobility technologies.
